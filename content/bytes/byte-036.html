Byte #036: Scope Creep and Model Overpromise — Where Projects Go Wrong

AI projects often promise the moon but deliver far less. Avoiding scope creep and overpromise is crucial for consultants.

Today’s Byte in a Nutshell:

Learn how to manage expectations and keep AI projects on track by understanding the pitfalls of scope creep and model overpromise.

Common Pitfalls:

1. Poorly defined project objectives leading to endless changes.
2. Overestimating model capabilities without considering data quality and business constraints.
3. Lack of stakeholder alignment causing shifting goals.

Setting Realistic Expectations:

Ensure clear communication of AI capabilities and limitations to stakeholders to prevent overpromise. Maintain a tight scope by aligning project goals with achievable outcomes.

Real-world Consulting Examples:

  • In a financial services project, a consultant prevented scope creep by setting milestone reviews and realigning the project scope with the client’s strategic goals.
  • A manufacturing firm avoided overpromise by conducting a thorough data readiness assessment before committing to advanced predictive modeling.
  • During a retail transformation project, consultants mitigated shifting goals by facilitating regular cross-departmental meetings to ensure alignment.

Why This Matters (to Us):

Avoiding scope creep and overpromise not only saves time and resources but also builds trust and credibility with clients by delivering on what was promised.

Consulting Tip:

Use a project charter to define boundaries and set clear expectations at the onset.
